About Wise Up

Wise Up is a platform for buying and selling textbooks in New Zealand. It is completly free to use and a smarter way to trade textbooks.

Wise Up was created after seeing the inefficient way students are trading used textbooks.

 

Online auction sites have expensive fees.

Using facebook is free but often there are issues with people missing eachothers posts and ever contacting eachother.

 The solution is Wise Up.
